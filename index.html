Privacy Policy for Rail Contacts

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

Rail Contacts ("we", "our", or "us") is a private community-based contacts application created specifically for railway running staff. This app is not an official Indian Railways application and is not affiliated with any government entity. It is designed to help staff easily connect with each other for work-related communication and coordination.

1. Who Can Use This App

  • Only verified railway running staff are permitted to use Rail Contacts.
  • Access requires login credentials, which are provided after verification.
  • Only designated admins can add new members or manage contacts.
  • Unauthorized users are not allowed to access the app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Basic contact details: name, phone number, designation, and related information.
  • Login credentials for authentication purposes.
  • We do not collect sensitive data like location, camera, SMS, or microphone access.

3. How We Use the Information

  • To maintain a private directory of railway running staff for communication purposes.
  • To help members quickly find and contact others in the same work domain.
  • Admins manage the data to keep it accurate and relevant.

4. Sharing of Information

  • Contact details are shared only within the verified community.
  • No data is shared with any external third party or made public.
  • All data is protected and accessible only to authenticated users.

5. Data Security

  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect user data.
  • Access is strictly controlled, and only verified users can view contact information.
  • Admins monitor and manage data access to prevent misuse.

6. Not an Official Railway App

Rail Contacts is a community initiative by railway staff and is not an official app of Indian Railways. It is intended for informal and operational coordination among railway running staff only.

7. User Responsibility

  • Users must use the app and its contact data solely for professional and respectful communication.
  • Sharing app data outside the platform is strictly prohibited.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified of any major changes through the app or via email.
